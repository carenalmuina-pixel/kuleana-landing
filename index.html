Types of Damage We Handle

A steady hand for every loss.

Storm-driven, water-borne, fire-charred or quietly hidden — we document what carriers leave out, so your settlement reflects the full scope of what you've lost.

Hurricane & Wind

Roof uplift, interior water intrusion, contents loss. We document scope before the carrier closes the file.

Water & Flood

Hidden moisture and substrate damage are the most under-paid line items in any claim. We find them.

Fire & Smoke

From soot remediation to total rebuild, we take our own measurements and build the Xactimate estimate ourselves, line by line — never a carrier-side number.

Mold & Moisture

Mold isn't a maintenance issue when it follows a covered loss. We tie cause to coverage in writing.

Hail & Storm

Granular loss on roofs, broken openings, screen damage — fully measured and matched to policy.

Frozen Plumbing

Burst pipes and resulting damage — documented before drywall is replaced and evidence is lost.

Theft & Vandalism

Inventoried contents, broken openings, repair scope — all on the record carriers respect.

Denied Claims

If your claim was denied, low-balled, or closed prematurely, we re-open it with a documented record.

If Your Claim Has Been…

Delayed. Underpaid. Denied.

You have the right to a second opinion. We step in at any point in the claim — even after a denial — and rebuild the record so your file can be heard fairly.

If your claim is delayed

Carriers have statutory timelines. We document every missed deadline, every unanswered email, and push the file to the front of the queue with the right paper trail.

If your claim is underpaid

We re-measure, re-investigate, and rebuild the Xactimate estimate line by line — confirmed against your policy, not the carrier's — and show, with photos and moisture readings, exactly the dollars left on the table.

If your claim is denied

A denial is a position, not a verdict. With a properly documented file we re-open the claim, address the cited exclusions, and bring it back to a fair recovery.

A word that carries both responsibility and privilege.

What People Ask Us

The questions
worth a real answer.

Pick up the phone, talk story, ask anything. There is no question too small when your home is involved.

"What does my policy actually cover?"

More than your carrier may let on. We read your declarations page, exclusions, and endorsements together — and translate the legalese into plain English so you know what you're owed.

"My claim was denied. Is it really over?"

Almost never. A denial is a position, not a verdict. With proper documentation — photos, moisture readings, a written narrative — most denied claims can be re-opened and recovered.

"Will hiring you slow my settlement down?"

The opposite. A complete, well-documented file moves faster through a carrier's queue than one they can quietly under-pay. We do the work that gets your claim taken seriously.

"What does this cost me up front?"

Nothing. The initial policy review is free, and we're only paid a small percentage of what we actually recover for you. If there's no recovery, there's no fee.
